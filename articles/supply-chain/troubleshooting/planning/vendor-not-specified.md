# Vendor isn't specified when planned orders are firmed

Error code: SYS23532

## Symptoms

When you try to firm planned orders, you receive the following error message:

> Vendor is not specified

## Resolution

To specify a vendor, follow these steps.

1. Open the planned order that is missing a vendor.
1. On the **Planned supply** FastTab, in the **Vendor** field, select a vendor.
