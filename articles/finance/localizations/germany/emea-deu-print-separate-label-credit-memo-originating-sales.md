# Print a separate label for credit memos that originate from sales

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

This article explains how to specify the label that appears on credit memos that originate from sales.

On the **Legal entities** page, use the **Print corrective invoice on a sales memo** option on the **Foreign trade and logistics** tab to specify the label that appears on credit memos that originate from sales (sales orders, non-item sales, and project sales).

- If you set the **Print corrective invoice on a sales memo** option to **No**, the label "Gutschrift" prints on all credit memos.
- If you set the **Print corrective invoice on a sales memo** option to **Yes**, the label "Rechnungskorrektur" prints on credit memos from sales orders, free text invoices, and project invoices.
