# Year-end close for Czech Republic and Hungary

[!include[banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

This article provides information about the year-end closing process and opening transactions for the Czech Republic and Hungary. By using this functionality, you can set up the year-end closing process in the following ways:

- Post to different accounts for closing and opening of balance accounts.
- Transfer the profit or loss year-end result into the newly opened year to a different account.

## Year-end closing setup

On the **Accounts for automatic transactions** page, you can specify the general ledger accounts to use during the year-end closing process:

- Year-end result – Use this account for transferring the ending balances of profit and loss transactions.
- Transfer of closing and opening transactions – Use this account for transferring ending balances of balance sheet accounts.

## Opening transactions setup

Before you can post opening transactions to a different account for each main account, specify the ledger account in the **Opening account** field on the main account.
