# Sales of zero-rated goods

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

1. Go to **Accounts receivable** \> **Sales orders** \> **All sales orders**.
1. Create a sales order for a zero-rated item.
1. Select the record, and then select **Tax information**.
1. On the **GST** FastTab, verify that the **Exempted** check box is selected.
1. Select the **Customer tax information** FastTab.
1. Select **OK**.
1. On the Action Pane, on the **Sell** tab, in the **Tax** group, select **Tax document**.
1. Select **Close**.

## Post the invoice

1. On the Action Pane, on the **Invoice tab**, in the **Generate** group, select **Invoice**.
1. In the **Quantity** field, select **All**.
1. Select **OK**.
1. Select **Yes** to acknowledge the warning message that you receive.

## Validate the voucher

1. On the Action Pane, on the **Invoice** tab, in the **Journals** group, select **Invoice**.
1. Select **Voucher**.
