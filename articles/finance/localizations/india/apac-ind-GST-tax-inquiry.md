# Tax inquiry

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

1. Go to **Tax** \> **Inquiries and reports** \> **Tax document transactions** \> **Posted tax document transactions**.
1. Select the registration number, and then select **OK**.

    :::image type="content" source="../media/Capture2019052111_upd.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Posted tax document transactions page.":::

1. Select **Voucher** to view the financial entry that is posted for the transaction.

    :::image type="content" source="../media/Capture2019052112_upd.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Voucher transactions page.":::

1. Select **Close**.
1. Select **Tax document** to view the tax that is calculated on the transaction.

    :::image type="content" source="../media/Capture2019052113_upd.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Tax document page.":::

1. Select **Close**.
1. Select **Posted sales tax**.

    :::image type="content" source="../media/Capture2019052114_upd.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Posted sales tax page.":::

1. Select **Close**.
