# Purchases from composite dealers

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

1. Go to **Accounts payable** > **Invoice** > **Invoice journals**.
1. Create a journal, and then select **Lines**.
1. Create a purchase transaction for a composite vendor, and save the record.
1. Select **Tax information**.

    :::image type="content" source="../media/Annotation-2019-05-16-101054.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Tax information dialog box.":::

1. On the **GST** FastTab, in the **HSN codes** field, select a value.

    :::image type="content" source="../media/Annotation-2019-05-16-101138.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the GST FastTab.":::

1. On the **Vendor tax information** FastTab, verify the information.

    :::image type="content" source="../media/Annotation-2019-05-16-101246.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Vendor tax information FastTab.":::

1. Select **OK**.

## Validate the tax details

1. Select **Tax document**.
1. Select **Close**.
1. Select **Post** > **Post** to post the journal.
1. Close the message that you receive.

## Validate a voucher

To validate a voucher, select **Inquiries** > **Voucher**.

:::image type="content" source="../media/Annotation-2019-05-16-100656.png" alt-text="Screenshot of an example voucher.":::
