## Employment to cdm_employments

LEGALENTITYID | = | cdm_companyid.cdm_companycode | 
WORKERTYPE | >> | cdm_workertype | 
EMPLOYMENTSTARTDATE | = | cdm_employmentstartdate | 
EMPLOYMENTENDDATE | = | cdm_employmentenddate |
